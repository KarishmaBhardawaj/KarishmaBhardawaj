Facts About My Kanhana

Krishna is known as the eighth incarnation of Lord Vishnu in Hinduism. He is considered to be one of the most widely revered and popular Indian divinities. Krishna is also known as the god of love, compassion, and tenderness, and his life is chronicled in the ancient Hindu epic, the Mahabharata. One of the most significant events in his life was the delivery of the Bhagavad Gita, which is a sacred text that contains his teachings on duty and spirituality. Krishna is often depicted in Hindu art and literature as a playful, romantic young boy or as a wise and powerful adult.

Krishna Other 6 Name

Lord Krishna is known by various names in Hinduism. Here are six of his other popular names:

1. Govinda

This name means "one who gives pleasure to cows" or "protector of cows.

2. Gopala

This name means "cowherd" or "protector of cows."

3. Shyam

This name means "dark" or "black" and is often used to refer to Lord Krishna's complexion

4. Hari

This name means "one who removes the sins" or "destroyer of evil.

5. Madhava

This name means "Lord of knowledge" or "Lord of honey.

6. Mukunda

This name means "one who grants liberation" or "giver of salvation.
